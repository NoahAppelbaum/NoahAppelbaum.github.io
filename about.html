Hi, I'm Noah!

I to solve problems creatively and to learn new skills. These passions have led me to building software and developing for web in JavaScript and Java.

Prior to pivoting into software development work, I spent many years as an actor, musician, and professional martial artist. Highlights of a wide-ranging career include performing motion capture for AAA video games, sword-fighting in front of thousands of people a night at the opera, and acting in a host of exciting and weird experimental theater productions.

In addition to performing, I have also taught as an instructor and curriculum developer for The Second City Training Center in Chicago, coached martial arts athletes to national-level medals, and worked with students of all ages learning to play musical instruments. I love sharing what I know, and helping others to realize their full potential!

I loved my Intro to Java Programming class in high school, and my interest in programming and general terminal inquisitiveness led me to completing an introductory Java MOOC through the University of Helsinki while working as a performer. (Including several assignments completed in the dressing rooms at The Lyric Opera.) With an eye towards web development, I also started learning JavaScript. Several years into this journey, I have turned my focus to full-time web and software development. I am currently enrolled in a full-stack development bootcamp with RITHM School, expecting to graduate in December of 2023.
